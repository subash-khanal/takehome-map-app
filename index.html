US Take-Home Pay Explorer

Enter annual gross income and tax status. Click states/counties on the map to estimate monthly take-home and optional cost-of-living adjusted value.

Click a state or county on the map to view details.
Cost-of-living adjustment formula: adjusted = raw monthly take-home * (100 / COL index).

Assumptions (MVP)

  • Federal tax model is simplified single-filer brackets.
  • State taxes use real state bracket data (Tax Foundation dataset).
  • Social Security and Medicare (FICA) are estimated.
  • County-level COL proxy uses HUD Fair Market Rent (2-bedroom) index.

Data Sources

  • US county boundaries (GeoJSON): Plotly Datasets
  • State income tax brackets (CSV): Tax Foundation Facts & Figures
  • County housing-cost proxy (2-bedroom FMR CSV): HUD Fair Market Rents
  • Federal income tax brackets (single filer baseline): IRS 2024 Tax Inflation Adjustments
  • FICA rates and wage base references: SSA Contribution and Benefit Base, IRS Additional Medicare Tax
  • County COL index is computed as: county 2-bedroom FMR / national median 2-bedroom FMR * 100.
